# The Floating Legend

![](../../public/features/legend.png)

A collapsible legend box is anchored to the bottom-right of the timeline canvas. It is always visible while you are in the Visualiser view.

## Legend sections

The legend contains four sections:

1. **Colour swatches** — the programmes, strategies, or statuses currently shown. Updates automatically when you change colour mode.
2. **Milestone types** — Info, Warning, and Critical milestone icons.
3. **Dependency arrows** — the three arrow styles (Blocks, Requires, Related) with their colours and arrowhead conventions.
4. **Conflict indicator** — a sample of the red conflict marker.

## Behaviour

- Click the legend header to collapse it to just the title bar. Click again to expand.
- The collapsed/expanded state persists across page reloads.
