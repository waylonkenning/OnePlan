# Zoom and Column Width

![Column resize](../../public/features/column-resize.png)

## Zooming the timeline

- Click the **+** button in the header to zoom in — columns become wider, giving more space for bar labels and fine-grained dates.
- Click the **−** button to zoom out — columns become narrower, fitting more of the portfolio into view.
- The zoom-in button is disabled at maximum zoom; the zoom-out button is disabled at minimum zoom.
- Zoom level persists across page reloads.

## Resizing the asset name column

- Drag the vertical border between the asset name sidebar and the timeline content area to resize it.
- Drag left to give more space to the timeline; drag right to give more space to long asset names.
- The resized width persists across page reloads.
