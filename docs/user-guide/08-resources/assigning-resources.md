# Assigning Resources

![Resource assignment checklist in the Initiative Panel](../../public/features/initiative-panel-resource-assignment.png)

Once your Resource Roster is populated, you can assign one or more people to any initiative. Assignments are stored in IndexedDB alongside all other initiative data and are reflected visually on the timeline bar when the Show Resources toggle is on.

## Assigning Resources to an Initiative

1. Click an initiative bar on the timeline to open the **Initiative Panel**.
2. Scroll to the **Resources** section in the panel.
3. Check the box next to each person you want to assign. All entries come from the Resource Roster.
4. Close the panel — assignments are saved automatically.

You can assign multiple resources to a single initiative. There is no limit on the number of assignees.

## Viewing Assigned Resources on the Timeline

1. Open **Display Settings** from the toolbar.
2. Enable the **Show Resources** toggle.

Assigned resource names appear directly on each initiative bar. If the bar is too narrow to show all names, they are truncated.

## Owner Initials Badge

The owner badge displayed on an initiative bar derives from the resource record linked as the initiative's owner. To set or change the owner, open the Initiative Panel and select a resource from the **Owner** field. The badge updates immediately to show that person's initials.
