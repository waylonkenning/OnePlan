# Moving and Resizing Initiatives

![Drag and resize](../../public/features/move-resize.png)

You can adjust initiative dates directly on the timeline by dragging. All changes persist immediately to IndexedDB — there is no Save button.

## Moving

Click once to select a bar, then drag it left or right. Both the start date and end date shift by the same number of days, preserving the initiative's duration. Changes are saved automatically on mouse release.

## Resizing from the Left

Drag the left edge of the bar to change the start date without affecting the end date. The initiative shortens or lengthens from the left.

## Resizing from the Right

Drag the right edge of the bar to change the end date without affecting the start date. The initiative shortens or lengthens from the right.

## Tap-to-Select First

A single click selects the bar and shows a highlight ring around it. Dragging only begins after selection. This prevents accidental moves when you just want to open a bar to view its details.
